Privacy Policy · Last updated: May 6, 2026

Short version: FocusFlow does not collect, store, or share any personal data. Everything stays on your device.

1. Information We Collect

FocusFlow does not collect any personal information. We do not require you to create an account, and we do not ask for your name, email address, or any other identifying information.

2. Data Storage

All data you create in FocusFlow — including your habits, reminders, focus sessions, and settings — is stored exclusively on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework. This data never leaves your device and is never transmitted to any server.

3. Analytics & Tracking

FocusFlow does not use any analytics tools, tracking SDKs, or third-party libraries that collect usage data. We do not track how you use the app.

4. Advertising

FocusFlow contains no advertisements and does not share any data with advertising networks.

5. Third-Party Services

FocusFlow does not integrate with any third-party services that would have access to your data.

6. Notifications

If you enable notifications, they are processed entirely on your device via Apple's notification system. We do not have access to your notification content or delivery status.

7. Children's Privacy

FocusFlow does not collect data from anyone, including children under the age of 13. The app is safe for all ages.

8. Changes to This Policy

If we make changes to this privacy policy, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
